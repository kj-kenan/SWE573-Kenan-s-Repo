About The Hive 🐝

The Hive is a community-driven platform built on kindness, cooperation, and the power of shared time. Members exchange services using Beellars — our own time-based community currency.

By joining The Hive, you contribute to a system where skills, empathy, and collaboration matter more than money. Together, we create a sustainable network of mutual support.
